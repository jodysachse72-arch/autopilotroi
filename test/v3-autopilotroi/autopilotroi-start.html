Step 1 · Complete
Get Trust Wallet
Your non-custodial crypto wallet — you own your keys
✓ Done
ℹ️
Why Trust Wallet? Aurum operates in DeFi — you need a self-custody wallet to move USDT into your account. Trust Wallet is free, takes 5 minutes, iOS & Android. You own your funds, nobody else has access.
📱
Download Trust Wallet
trustwallet.com · iOS & Android · Free
→
1
Download from App Store or Google Play
Blue shield icon. Only from official app stores — never a third-party link.
2
Create wallet & write down your 12-word seed phrase
This is your master key. Write it on paper, store safely. Never share with anyone — ever.
⚠️ Lose this = lose your funds permanently. No recovery exists.
3
Enable USDT (TRC-20) in your wallet
Search USDT in the token list and add TRC-20. This is the version Aurum uses for deposits.

Step 2 · Complete
Set Up Your VPN
Required for US & Canada users to access Aurum
✓ Done
⚠️
US & Canada users: Aurum requires a VPN while US regulatory licensing is finalized. Route through UK or Mexico — both work reliably. Legal and widely used. Outside US/Canada? Skip straight to Step 3.
1
Download NordVPN or ExpressVPN
Both work for Aurum. NordVPN has a trial period to test first.
2
Connect to a UK or Mexico server
UK servers are most stable for Aurum. Keep VPN on during all setup and transactions.
💡 Keep VPN active during account creation and all deposits.

Step 3 · You Are Here
Get USDT into Trust Wallet
Fund your wallet before creating your Aurum account
In Progress
ℹ️
Why USDT? Aurum deposits use USDT (Tether) — a stablecoin pegged to USD. It doesn't fluctuate like Bitcoin. Minimum deposit is $100 USDT. Have your amount ready before creating your account.
1
Buy USDT on Coinbase, Kraken, or Binance
Create account, verify identity, purchase your USDT amount. Minimum $100.
2
Send USDT to your Trust Wallet (TRC-20 address)
Copy your Trust Wallet TRC-20 address. Paste it in the exchange withdrawal. Double-check before confirming.
⚠️ Always send a $1–$5 test transaction first to confirm the address.
3
Wait for confirmation (5–15 minutes)
TRC-20 is fast. USDT appears in Trust Wallet once confirmed. Ready for Step 4.
